Preferred vs Non-preferred, whats the difference?

Its a fancy way of saying in vs out of network providers.

If you use an in-network doctor or other health care provider, this plan will pay some or all of the costs of covered services. Be aware, your in-network doctor or hospital may use an out-of-network provider for some services. Plans use the term in-network, preferred, or participating for providers in their network.
